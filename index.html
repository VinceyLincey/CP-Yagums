What We Offer

Everything your campus needs, in one place

YAGUMS brings together facility management, maintenance tracking, and team coordination into a single, unified platform.

🏛️
Facility Booking

Reserve lecture halls, computer labs, sports courts, meeting rooms, and study spaces with real-time availability. Conflict detection prevents double-bookings automatically.

✦ Core Feature
🔧
Maintenance Tracking

Submit, prioritise, and monitor repair requests from anywhere on campus. Maintenance staff get assigned tasks automatically.

🔔
Smart Notifications

Stay informed with real-time alerts for booking approvals, maintenance updates, and task assignments.

👥
Role-Based Access

Five distinct roles — Student, Lecturer, Facility Manager, Maintenance Staff, and System Admin — each with tailored permissions.

📊
Dashboard Overview

A unified dashboard showing all bookings, requests, and notifications at a glance, personalised for your role.
